Andrew is a spatial ecologist interested in conservation in multi-functional landscapes. He is interested in threatened species conservation, community ecology, invasive species dynamics, and urban ecology. Andrew has worked across industry, NGOs and Universities in a mix of field and research roles. His current work is on Biodiversity and Energy System Planning which is a part of the Net Zero Australia phase 2 research program. This work is a collaboration between the Melbourne Energy Institute and the Melbourne Biodiversity Institute at the University of Melbourne.
